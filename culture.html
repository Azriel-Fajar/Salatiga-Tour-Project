A Glimpse into Our Heritage

Salatiga is a city rich in cultural heritage, offering a unique blend of traditional arts, historical landmarks, and vibrant customs. From the intricate movements of our traditional dances to the timeless stories etched in our ancient temples, every corner of Salatiga tells a story of a proud and resilient culture.

Traditional Arts

Experience the beauty of Wayang kulit, Gamelan music, and traditional dances that have been passed down through generations.

Historical Sites

Visit ancient temples, colonial-era buildings, and museums that tell the story of Salatiga's rich and diverse history.

Local Customs

Immerse yourself in the local way of life by participating in traditional ceremonies and community festivals.
